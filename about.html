Situated in Urun-Islampur, within the Walwa Taluka of the Sangli district in Southern Maharashtra, lies Yashwantrao Chavan College.In pursuit of "Bahujan Hitay, Bahujan Sukhay" (for the good of many, for the prosperity of many), the late Shri. S. D. Patil(ex-member of parliament), an educationalist and social reformer, created it in June 1970. April 1976 saw the college's UGC recognition under 20.5f, and June 2005 saw its permanent affiliation with Shivaji University, Kolhapur. From the college's founding it has provided invaluable assistance in the areas of education, athletics, NSS, and cultural events. At the graduate level, it offers instruction in business administration, computer science, science, arts, and commerce. Additionally, it offers a large number of timely Career Orientated Courses (COCs) from Shivaji University in Kolhapur and UGC. In order to improve education and the welfare of the community, the late Shri. S. D. Patil and his associates established the Walwa Taluka Education Society in 1945. In Southern Maharashtra, the late Shri. S. D. Patil was highly involved in both social and political affairs. He was chosen from the Sangli area to serve as the M.L.A., M.P., and President of Zillah Parishad.
